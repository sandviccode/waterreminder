Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Data Collection

Water Reminder app stores your water consumption logs and app settings locally on your device. This data is not sent to our servers. However, third-party providers such as Google AdMob for advertising services and RevenueCat for purchase management may collect device identifiers and usage data.

Data Usage

App data (water consumption logs, reminder settings, personalization preferences) is only used to provide app functionality and remains on your device. Data collected for advertising purposes may be used to show you relevant ads. Purchase data is processed by RevenueCat to manage your subscriptions and purchases.

Data Storage

Your app data is stored in your device\'s local storage (AsyncStorage). When you uninstall the app, this data is automatically deleted. Data collected by third-party providers (Google AdMob, RevenueCat) is subject to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

Your in-app data (water consumption, settings) is not shared with third parties. However, Google AdMob may process device information for ad delivery, and RevenueCat may process device information to manage your purchases.

Advertising & Tracking

Our app displays ads through Google AdMob. Depending on your consent, you may see personalized or non-personalized ads. For personalized ads, device identifiers, IP address, and app usage data may be processed. You can change your advertising preferences at any time through your device settings.

Purchase Services

In-app purchases are managed through RevenueCat. RevenueCat collects data such as device identifiers, purchase information, and IP address to process your purchases, manage your subscriptions, and verify your purchase history. This data is synchronized with Apple/Google payment systems.

Notifications

Water reminder notifications work entirely locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers for notifications. Your notification settings are stored on your device and are only used to send you reminders.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

You can access the privacy policies of the third-party services we use through the following links:

  • Google AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat: revenuecat.com/privacy

User Rights

You have full control over your app data. You can delete your local data by uninstalling the app. You can manage your ad personalization preferences through device settings or by using the ad consent form within the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, you can contact us at sandviccode@gmail.com.
